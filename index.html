San Francisco Zombie Walk

Saturday October 24, 2015

About

The zombies awaken. The spirits of the dead rise to invade the streets of San Francisco.

The San Francisco Zombie Walk is a yearly free event open to people of all ages and backgrounds.

The ultimate goal is absolute zombie domination :)

Whenever we can, we will work with non-profit organizations to raise social awareness in our community and to help those in need.

2015 Walk

When: Saturday October 24, 2015 - 2:00 PM

Where: Jane Warner Plaza - 17th and Market Street

ALL AGES - NO COVER

With the San Francisco - Marin Food Bank - collecting canned food for those in need; bring your goodies!!

Points of interest at the walk include:

  • Castro Street
  • The Castro Theatre
  • Roxie Theatre
  • Valencia Street
  • Mission Street
  • Dolores Park

And many more!

SF-Marin Food Bank



With the mission to end hunger in San Francisco and Marin, the San Francisco - Marin Food Bank employs 130 people and 25,000 volunteers each year; boasting 450 partner organizations throughout the community.



We are very proud to support such wonderful organization! Bring your canned food at the San Francisco Zombie Walk and help with a great cause.
